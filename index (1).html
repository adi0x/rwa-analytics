Act 04 ‚Äî The Risks

Not Without Its Challenges

Tokenization isn't magic. Understanding the risks is what separates informed conviction from blind hype.

üîê Smart Contract Risk MEDIUM

Bugs in code can lead to exploits. Most top RWA protocols are audited, but risk is never zero.

‚öñÔ∏è Regulatory Risk HIGH

Laws vary wildly by jurisdiction. SEC, MiCA, and Asian frameworks still evolving. Compliance is fragmented.

üè¶ Counterparty Risk MEDIUM

Someone holds the real assets off-chain. Custodian trust and transparency vary across protocols.

üíß Liquidity Risk HIGH

Most tokenized assets have thin secondary markets. Low trading volumes and long holding periods remain a bottleneck.

üîó Interoperability Risk MEDIUM

Assets fragmented across chains. No universal standard yet for cross-chain RWA transfers.

üìã Ownership Verification MEDIUM

Proving a token represents real ownership of an off-chain asset remains legally complex in many jurisdictions.
